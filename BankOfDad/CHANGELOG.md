# Bank of Dad v2.2.0

## Product update

This update moves Bank of Dad toward the full product system while keeping the functional Arduino/LVGL structure intact.

## Added / changed

- Expanded UK household chore economy to 27 chores.
- Maximum chore reward is capped at £4.50.
- Added XP scaling based on chore value.
- Chore requests now persist pending state through NVS.
- Chore requests now push notifications.
- Approval queue remains Dad-controlled.
- Reward count expanded from 8 to 10.
- Added McDonald's and Cinema Trip reward slots.
- Chore and reward panels are prepared for longer scrollable lists.
- Platform version bumped to 2.2.0.
